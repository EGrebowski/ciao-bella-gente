About Leona

Having immigrated from Italy in 2010, Leona arrived in America as a young woman full of energy and the determination to blend into American culture. But anyone who meets Leona would instantly agree that she has wonderfully done so without compromising her unique traditional qualities. She’s fluent in 4 languages and spent the first stages of her career as a translator in both personal and business settings, but this hardly begins to capture her expertise. Leona’s students love their lessons, look forward to every session, and reach their goals - whether they be for communicating with family, preparing for travel, launching an international business, citizenship, or expressing love and romance (even wedding vows!) with the most passionate words.


Read the testimonials from her students, purely their words, and decide for yourself if they are expressing a gratitude that’s simply not common in language lessons. The feelings are mutual. Leona adores her students, now good friends one and all.

Italian Lessons

Unique Lesson Style

The key is flexibility, energy, and a comfortable environment. Leona meets her students in convenient locations that help them relax… coffee shops, bookstores, happy hours, and Skype when business and home obligations render a face-to-face difficult. Singles, couples, and group rates are available, weekday evenings and Saturday mornings. Lessons are highly conversational with an emphasis on proper grammar and recitation until pronunciation and inflection are correct. They read topical articles, talk about their days and families, and hear all about favorite Italian places and customs with tips for getting around town…. in a mix of Italian and English that changes as students grow more confident.

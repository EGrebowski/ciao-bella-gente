Programs

Group Language Courses

Italian Club, Ybor City

For those interested in becoming conversational, this multi-level program teaches true Italian in a high-energy, low pressure style. Beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels are offered via a custom-built 32-lesson curriculum that blends vocabulary, grammar, and culture from a simple English-speaking perspective. To date, over 750 students have journeyed through these 8-week semesters at their own pace, offered three times per year. Low cost, high fun, lasting friendships, and wine!

Conversational Book & Movie Club

Italian Club, Ybor City

For the fluent and near-fluent, this advanced program is an opportunity to perfect Italian conversational skills through lively literary and movie discussions while bonding with people of similar goals. An 8-week series is offered three times per year in our casual yet energetic setting at the Italian Club in Ybor. Classes are conducted almost purely in Italian, with students leading much of the book and movie discussions.

Travel Courses

Sarasota, Tampa, & St Pete Beach

Get ready for your next Italian adventure with a 4-lesson crash course! Learn essential travel phrases, cultural tips, and proper pronunciation. Practice through fun role-play scenarios in low-pressure retail, restaurant, and train station settings. These courses are perfect for enhancing your upcoming travel experiences with confident and authentic interaction.

Overseas Total Immersion Trips

Select Regions of Italy

The inaugural trip took place in Puglia, Sept 2025, with more to follow as interest warrants. Coordinated by an experienced travel partner, 11 advanced students enjoyed a full immersion in a quaint Italian-speaking village, including cultural excursions and UNESCO sites in the surrounding area. This and future travel packages include lodging, meals, unique events, and lessons… everything you’d want for an unforgettable Italian experience.

Elegant Dinner Entertainment Experiences

The Epicurean Hotel, Tampa, FL

Eat, Drink, & Speak Italian! These unforgettable evenings blend language & cultural exposure, gourmet cuisine, fine wine, and cooking tips. Held at the beautifully-boutique Epicurean Hotel and hosted by a master chef /sommelier / Leona trio, these highly-entertaining evenings are a unique fusion of instruction and entertainment you won't find anywhere else, perfect for public, private, and corporate audiences.

About Leona

Leona emigrated from Rome to America in 2011 and soon after began giving individual and small group lessons in the Tampa area with a very unique and energetic style. She launched her company Ciao Bella Gente! in 2018 and created a popular classroom program at the Italian Club in Ybor City which has grown in scope over seven years, now spanning five levels of true language & grammar instruction. Several years ago she expanded by adding shorter interactive courses focused on preparing participants for upcoming travel, with emphasis on useful phrases instead, partnering with retail locations in Tampa, Sarasota, and St. Pete Beach. Leona is known for her ability to connect with people, generate enthusiasm in every group, and create bonds of friendship that last well after classes end. Recently she appeared on WFLA’s popular morning program Daytime, and previously was recognized as a 2019 Woman of Excellence by the Ladies Auxiliary of the Italian Club for her contribution and dedication toward expanding Italian culture into the Tampa community.

My late father told me once that, as a child, I spoke Italian fluently, better than any member of my family. My maternal grandparents spoke no English but I was able to communicate with them effortlessly. That all changed when my father realized that kindergarten was starting very soon, but I spoke no English. So, everyone spoke only English around me and stopped speaking Italian. As a result, as time progressed, I forgot how to speak Italian.


Fast forward to adulthood. Whenever I would hear Italian spoken, or an Italian song, or would read something with an Italian sentence, it would stir something deep inside me. This was especially true as my wife and I would make many trips to Italy where the language was all around me. On one such trip to Salerno, I resolved that I would learn Italian again.


I signed up for Level 1 with Leona at the Italian Club in Ybor City. I was hooked. I couldn't wait for each class. I began to take weekly Private Lessons with her. I found her to be a patient, kind teacher. I especially enjoyed when she would speak to me in Italian during the lesson, and I was delighted that I began to understand her! Her dedication to her students was paying off for me.


As her classroom time increased, she referred me to her niece Flavia for Private Lessons. Flavia lives in Assisi, Italy and follows her Aunt Leona's curriculum to the letter. I study with her weekly online. I could not be happier with my progress.


I'm one year plus in regarding my lessons. Am I fluent? No, but I am able to read, understand, and speak Italian much better than when I started. I am compelled to keep going with my lessons and will not stop until I am fluent. With such teachers as Leona and Flavia, I know I am in good hands. To those of you reading this and considering studying Italian with Leona, I say "Iniziamo!" Let's Begin!

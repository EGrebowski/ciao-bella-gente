About Leona

Having grown up in Rome, Leona immigrated to the United States in 2010. She has extensive experience as a translator, is fluent in four languages, and has been teaching Italian for more than seven years. Leona’s students love their lessons, look forward to every session, and, most importantly, reach their goals—whether they be to communicate with family, prepare for travel, launch an international business, attain Italian citizenship, or express love (even wedding vows!) with the most passionate words.

Italian Lessons

Unique Lesson Style

The keys to success are flexibility, energy, and a comfortable environment. Leona meets her students in convenient locations that help them relax—coffee shops, bookstores, happy hours, and even Skype when a face-to-face is difficult. Singles, couples, and group rates are available for weekday evenings and Saturday mornings. Lessons are highly conversational with an emphasis on proper grammar and recitation until pronunciation and inflection are correct. Students read topical articles, discuss their days and families, and hear all about Italian places and customs with tips for getting around town…in a combination of Italian and English that changes as students grow more confident.

About Leona

Having grown up in Rome, Leona immigrated to the United States in 2010. Although fluent in four languages, Italian is her greatest love. She has experience working as a translator, and has been teaching Italian to eager students for more than seven years now. Leona’s students love their lessons, look forward to every session, and, most importantly, reach their goals—whether they be communicating with family, preparing for travel, launching an international business, working towards Italian citizenship, or expressing love (even wedding vows!) with the most passionate words.


Read the testimonials from her students to see how they achieved their goals as they developed a lasting friendship and gained a passion for the Italian language.

Italian Lessons

Unique Lesson Style

The key is flexibility, energy, and a comfortable environment. Leona meets her students in convenient locations that help them relax—coffee shops, bookstores, happy hours, and even Skype when a face-to-face is difficult. Singles, couples, and group rates are available, weekday evenings and Saturday mornings. Lessons are highly conversational with an emphasis on proper grammar and recitation until pronunciation and inflection are correct. Students read topical articles, talk about their days and families, and hear all about Italian places and customs with tips for getting around town…in a combination of Italian and English that changes as students grow more confident.
